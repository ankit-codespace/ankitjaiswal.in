Privacy Policy for "I Love Notepad"

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application I Love Notepad ("Application") for desktop devices, created by Ankit Jaiswal.

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided & Automatically Collected Information:

The Application does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or user files. All text files and notepad documents you create, edit, or open using the Application are stored strictly locally on your own personal device. No notes or content are ever sent to any remote servers.

2. Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

No. This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your device.

3. Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No. Since the Application operates completely offline and does not collect any data, no information is shared with or visible to any third parties.

4. Children

The Application does not collect any data and is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at contact@ankitjaiswal.in or visit ankitjaiswal.in.
